When was No Limit Records started?
1991
After the death of his grandfather, Miller inherited $10,000 as part of a malpractice settlement. Miller opened a record store in Richmond, California called No Limit Records And Tapes, which later became the foundation for No Limit Records. Beginning in 1991, the label released albums by Master P and the group TRU (The Real Untouchables), a trio consisting of Miller and his brothers, Corey and Vyshonn, who also recorded as “C-Murder” and “Silkk the Shocker,” respectively.

Did Master P play for the National Basketball Association?
Master P had two stints with an NBA team. Master P played for the Charlotte Hornets in the 1998 pre-season. He also played for the Tornoto Raptors in the 1999 pre-season. However, Master P's contract was not extend past pre-season by either team and he never played a regular season game as an NBA player. These two opportunities came to him when he was already in his 30s.

Was Master P the first rapper to secure 100% ownership of his masters?
Miller was the first rapper to make $100 million and the first rap star to retain full ownership of his masters. He pioneered the artist-as-businessman prototype so prevalent in hip-hop today. After turning his Bay Area retail shop No Limit Records into an independent label in the early 1990s, P moved his operation to his native Louisiana and, with a distribution deal through Priority Records, established a gangsta-rap dynasty that had the whole world saying his trademark “Ugghhh!” In 1998, he earned an estimated $56.5 million, which landed him in the top 10 of Forbes magazines' list of highest-paid entertainers of the year.

Has Master P authored any publications?
Percy Miller, aka Master P, penned the book "Guaranteed Success" which was published in 2007. He shares his personal blueprint to a successful life. The notorious rapper explains how to achieve goals, manage money, and live a prosperous and purposeful life. Miller is living proof of the truth and power of that statement. What films has Master P been in?
Master P has been in over 30 films, from minor to starring roles. His most popular roles was in the 1998 comedy I Got the Hook-Up starring Anthony Johnson, Master P, Ice Cube, C-Murder and directed by Michael Martin. This was No Limit Records' first theatrical release. The movie was distributed by Dimension Films. It went on to earn $10.3 million in box office sales.
