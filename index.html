The Man Behind No Limit Records

Percy Miller, aka Master P, was born April 29, 1970 in New Orleans. The rapper, actor, and entrepreneur is also the founder of the record label No Limit Records. Before he rose to fame in the 90s, he faced much adversity. Miller along with his four siblings grew up in a single-parent home in a neighborhood riddled with crime. Seeing his family struggle to make ends meet motivated the rapper to strive for success. No one in his family could find work, so he created his own job.

"I was so hungry that my stomach was literally cramping."

In high school, he started selling cell phones as his first business venture. He also was a star player on the basketball team, garnering him a scholarship to college to major in Business. He soon dropped out, but after inheriting money due to the death of his grandfather, he opened a record store, No Limit Records & Tapes.

From Humble Beginnings to Stardom

The store grew into the record label No Limit Records and, under the monicker Master P, Miller released his first cassette tape in 1990. Unfortunately, he suffered another loss of a family member soon after. This motivated Miller even more to become a great success.

He branched out from being a rapper, exploring other business ventures. He was one of the first rappers to take advantage of the retail potential in the music industry. He invested in many businesses, such as Foot Locker, real estate, and television production. He even starred in a television show with his son, Romeo Miller. He went on to own more record labels, a film company, a clothing line, and the popular snack brand, Rap Snacks. Master P is the ultimate businessman, something his younger self would be proud of.

Discography

Aside from being a successful businessman, Master P is also a legend in Hip-Hop. The rapper has sold over 7 million albums during the span of his career. His discography includes fourteen studio albums, 40 singles, 16 compilation albums, one collaboration ablum, and 33 music videos.

His album Ghetto D was his first to reach number one on the Billboard Charts, also garnering a triple platinum certification. The album contains one of his most popular songs, "Make 'Em Say Uhh!" which by itself went platinum. Master P's most recent album Empire, from the Hood to Hollywood was released in 2015.
